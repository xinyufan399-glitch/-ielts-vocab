Part 3 讨论与拓展

Science科学

Part 3
What is the influence of science on human life?
Science has transformed daily life by making ordinary activities safer, faster, and more efficient. From medicine to transport to communication, scientific progress has extended life expectancy and expanded what people can do in a normal day.
How can ordinary people contribute to scientific research?
Ordinary people can contribute through public awareness, citizen science projects, and financial support. Even simple actions like sharing accurate information or participating in data-collection projects can be meaningful.
Why is international cooperation in science important?
Because major problems such as pandemics and climate change do not stop at national borders. Cooperation allows countries to share data, resources, and expertise, which usually speeds up progress and improves outcomes.
万能结构：观点 → 例子 → broader impact

Plans计划

Part 3
Is making plans important?
Yes, because plans give people direction and reduce uncertainty. A clear plan helps people allocate their time and energy more effectively instead of reacting to everything at the last minute.
What kinds of activities need careful planning?
Activities involving money, travel, deadlines, or coordination with other people usually need careful planning. The more moving parts there are, the more important planning becomes.
Can over-planning be a problem?
Yes, sometimes. If people plan every detail too rigidly, they may struggle to adapt when reality changes. So planning is useful, but flexibility matters just as much.
万能结构：clear stance → why → balance

Leadership and Encouragement领导与鼓励

Part 3
How can leaders encourage employees?
Good leaders encourage employees by recognizing effort, giving useful feedback, and creating an atmosphere where people feel trusted. Motivation grows when workers feel that their work is noticed and meaningful.
Why do some people need encouragement more than others?
Because confidence levels vary from person to person. Some people are naturally self-driven, while others need external reassurance, especially when they are facing pressure or unfamiliar tasks.
Is praise always effective?
Not always. Praise works best when it is specific and sincere. If it feels exaggerated or empty, people may stop taking it seriously.
万能结构：method → reason → qualification

Apology and Responsibility道歉与责任

Part 3
Why do some people dislike apologizing?
Some people associate apologizing with weakness or loss of pride. Others simply feel uncomfortable admitting fault, especially in situations where they want to protect their image.
Are words more important than actions?
Usually actions matter more, because they show real commitment. However, words still have value when they are honest and followed by consistent behavior.
Does admitting mistakes help relationships?
In many cases, yes. Taking responsibility can rebuild trust because it shows maturity, honesty, and respect for the other person’s feelings.
万能结构：explain motive → compare → conclude

Part 3
Why are toys important for children?
Toys support children’s development by encouraging imagination, interaction, and fine motor skills. They can also provide comfort and help children make sense of the world through play.

Toys that encourage creativity, movement, or social interaction are often the most beneficial. Open-ended toys are especially valuable because children can use them in many different ways.

Part 3
What kinds of goods are produced in modern factories?
Modern factories produce a huge range of items, including electronics, vehicles, food products, clothes, and medicine. The key feature is large-scale, standardized production.
Are machines better than handmade production?
Machines are better for efficiency and consistency, especially in mass production. Handmade work, however, still has value in areas where craftsmanship, uniqueness, or artistic meaning matters.
Will factories become more automated in the future?
Yes, most likely. Automation can improve speed and accuracy, but I don’t think humans will disappear completely because oversight, creativity, and problem-solving are still essential.
万能结构：main trend → contrast → future view

Part 3

People visit famous buildings because they are interested in history, design, and cultural meaning. A well-known building often represents more than itself; it can symbolize a city or a period of time.

Definitely. Modern buildings often feel efficient and dynamic, while old buildings tend to feel more distinctive and emotionally meaningful. Each type has its own appeal.

Animals and Tourism动物与旅游

Part 3
How can children learn about animals?
Children can learn through books, documentaries, school activities, and visits to zoos or wildlife parks. Direct observation often makes knowledge more vivid and memorable.
Does tourism have a positive or negative effect on animals?
It can have both. Tourism may raise awareness and provide funding for protection, but poorly managed tourism can also damage habitats and stress animals.
Why should people protect animals?
Because animals are part of the natural balance of the planet, and many species are vulnerable to human activity. Protecting them is both an ecological and an ethical responsibility.
